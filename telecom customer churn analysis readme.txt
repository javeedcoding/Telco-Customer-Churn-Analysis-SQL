project: telco customer churn Analysis
key insights:

overall churn rate is 26.58% indicating high customer attrition.

2. month to month contracts show the highest churn(42.71%) 
   suggesting lack of long-term commitment increases churn
   risk.

3. fiber optic users have high churn(42%), potentially due
   to pricing or service expectations.
           
4. customers in first 12 months show highest churn (47%)
   meaning early-stage retention is critical.

5. customers with higher monthly charges churn more indicating price    
   sensitivity.
